I am deeply passionate about experiences that infiltrate and affect our daily lives, particularly the relationship between our experience at work and our well-being. I believe one of the most impactful leverage points to our well-being at work stems from the presence of conscious leadership. At Mavrck at Chief of Staff I currently focus on evolving conscious leadership, on scaling our culture, and reducing the distance between aspirational and practiced values.

With extensive experience in consulting, business strategy, and experience design I bring a people-first lens to building human-centered, sustainable experiences.

In my work and throughout my life, I aim to learn more with each passing year than I learned the previous year. I am always looking for outlets to fuel this curiosity!
