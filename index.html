Hi, I'm Nalani!
 
 

Value-centered thinker,
experience strategist, and
DIY craft enthusiast. 

 
 
 

I am passionate about improving people's lives through informed problem solving and value-centered design.

Value-centered design acknowledges the two way street between customers and organizations. I am passionate about this intersection of business + design, and my goal is to work toward bridging the gap between business conversations and design conversations. Advocating for the customer experience while understanding and delivering on business requirements and goals is a vantage point I think is too often overlooked, and is an area in which I am passionate about delivering value.

With a business background and a passion for the power of design as a problem solving method, I am committed to sustainably innovating with a focus on human experiences.

In my work and throughout my life, I aim to learn more with each passing year than I learned the previous year. I am always looking for outlets to fuel this curiosity and desire to learn!
